About us

A strong technical solution starts with the right design

McPumps is a family-owned company focused on sales, delivery and consultation in pumping technology and water management.

Our experience covers drinking water supply, wastewater transport and treatment, pump monitoring, predictive maintenance and optimisation of operating costs.

  • We recommend solutions based on real operating conditions, not a generic catalogue answer.
  • We combine municipal and industrial water management know-how with practical solutions for households and smaller facilities.
  • We support customers in technology selection, delivery and further system development.
